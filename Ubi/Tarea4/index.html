About BRS

Mission

To quote https://24hoursofcycling.com/:
“Mountain biking shouldn’t just be for some freaks. Mountain biking should be for all freaks.”

Vision

The Bike Races Series org aspires to organize trials and routes in all the mountains in Europe, available to amateur bikers that love travel and adventure.

General Information

The Bike Races Series is an organization that promotes trials and routes for amateur bikers in different mountains around Europe. We want to organize events across multiple countries.
